WHO WE ARE

We are a Christ-centered church committed to sharing God’s love, building strong families, and transforming lives through faith in Jesus Christ. Our church is a welcoming community where people of all ages and backgrounds can grow spiritually and discover their purpose.

MISSION

Our mission is to:

    Connect people to our Saviour Jesus Christ, Nurture Faith and Grow together ig godliness.

VISION

Our vision is to be a vibrant church connecting people to Jesus and makinh Him known

CORE VALUES

  • BIBLICAL AUTHORITY:We acknowledge the word of God as the final authority.
  • PRAYER:We commot to pray for ourselves, those we disciple and the church.
  • SACRIFICIAL LOVE:We commit ourselves to show love to God even at the point of sacrifice.
  • GENEROSITY:We commit to be grnerous with all the blessings that God gives us.
  • DEPENDANCE ON GOD:We trust fully in God`s wisdom, strength and provision, acknowledging that without Him we can do nothing.

CHURCH HISTORY

Founded in 1989, our church began with a small group of believers meeting in a home. Today, we are a growing family dedicated to spreading hope and faith in our community.

WHAT MAKES US DIFFERENT

We believe in passionate worship, practical teaching, and real community. We are not just a church you attend — we are a family you belong to.

OUR IMPACT / TESTIMONIES

Through God’s grace, lives have been transformed, families restored, and hope renewed. We continue to see testimonies of healing, salvation, and spiritual growth.
